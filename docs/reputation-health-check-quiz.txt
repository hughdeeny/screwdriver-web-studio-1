REPUTATION HEALTH CHECK QUIZ — CURRENT SPEC
==========================================

Flow (3 steps):
1. Scored questions Q1–Q10
2. Situation questions Q11–Q15 (Q15 optional open text)
3. Contact form → in-page results

Public results show ONLY:
- Overall Reputation Health Score /100
- Score category
- Short intro paragraph
- "Your current system" — 3 analysis points
- "Recommended next steps" — 2–3 steps
- Recommended solution + dynamic CTA

NOT shown publicly:
- Trust / Visibility / Revenue scores or percentages
- Raw pillar calculations
- "Conversion" label

---

SCORED QUESTIONS (Q1–Q10)
Scoring: Strong=10, Good/manual=7, Weak=4, None/not sure=0

Q1. Do you ask customers for a review after a job is completed?
  Internal pillar: Revenue

Q2. Are review requests sent while the customer experience is still fresh?
  Internal pillar: Revenue

Q3. Do you make it easy for customers to leave a review with a Google review link?
  Internal pillar: Trust

Q4. Do happy customers usually get a reminder if they forget to leave a review?
  Internal pillar: Revenue

Q5. Do you reply to Google reviews in a way that makes future customers trust you more?
  Internal pillar: Trust

Q6. Do you have a list of past happy customers you could contact for a review request?
  Internal pillar: Revenue

Q7. If you received a negative or unfair review, would you know exactly how to respond?
  Internal pillar: Trust

Q8. Do you use positive reviews or customer feedback as social proof on your website or social media?
  Internal pillar: Visibility

Q9. Do you know whether your Google Business Profile is generating calls, website visits, or enquiries?
  Internal pillar: Visibility

Q10. Does your review process still happen when the business gets busy?
  Internal pillar: Revenue

---

UNSCORED QUESTIONS (Q11–Q15)

Q11. Which best describes your current situation?
Q12. Which desired outcome would you want to achieve in the next 90 days?
Q13. What do you think is the biggest obstacle getting in your way?
Q14. Which type of solution would suit you best?
Q15. Is there anything else you think we should know about? (optional open text)

---

SCORING

Total = Q1 + Q2 + ... + Q10 (max 100)

Score categories:
  >= 80  → Strong reputation system
  >= 60  → Good foundation, but room to improve
  >= 40  → Clear reputation growth opportunity
  <  40  → Reputation system needs attention

Internal pillars (not displayed):
  Trust:      Q3 + Q5 + Q7 (max 30)
  Visibility: Q8 + Q9 (max 20)
  Revenue:    Q1 + Q2 + Q4 + Q6 + Q10 (max 50)

  trustPercent      = round((trust / 30) * 100)
  visibilityPercent = round((visibility / 20) * 100)
  revenuePercent    = round((revenue / 50) * 100)

  lowestPillar    = pillar with lowest percentage
  strongestPillar = pillar with highest percentage

---

RESULT GENERATION

Analysis points (exactly 3): priority = lowest pillar → Q13 → Q12 → Q11 → strongest pillar
Next steps (2–3): priority = Q12 → Q13 → lowest pillar → fallbacks
Recommended solution + CTA + leadType: based on Q14
